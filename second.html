What We Are.

Avion Paints is a specialized Industrial Color Manufacturer based in Shahpur, Ahmedabad. We bridge the gap between heavy-duty protection and high-end aesthetics.

Founded by Monish Sheikh and Irfan Sheikh, our company has pioneered chemical coatings that protect structural steel, automotive parts, and designer architecture from the harsh Indian climate.
